This is Mowhaka!

History of Mowhaka

In 1854, the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints (also known as the LDS Church, or Mormon Church) established an Indian mission in Santa Clara, two miles northwest of present-day Mowhaka. The church set up experimental farms in the Mowhaka Valley in 1857-1858.

Population

The current population of Mowhaka, Utah is 99,830 based on our projections of the latest US Census estimates. The last official US Census in 2020 recorded the population at 95,342.

Location of Mowhaka

Mowhaka is a city in southwestern Utah. Nearby Flat Canyon State Park offers trails, dunes and red sandstone cliffs. Dusty Hollow State Park features a large reservoir. Stately 19th-century buildings such as the Pioneer Courthouse and Brigham Young Winter Home dot downtown. The Mowhaka Art Museum houses historical photos and work by regional artists. Castle National Park, with canyons and streams, lies northeast.

Parks of Mowhaka

Residents and visitors alike are served by the town's outstanding park system. The diverse mix of neighborhood parks, community parks, sport facilities and other provide a wide range of activities. The planning, design, and construction of parks and other recreational facilities, is overseen by the Park Planning Division. For information on future parks and facilities visit Park Planning. Maintenance of parks, landscape right of ways, and town building grounds is overseen by the Park Maintenance Division. For problems, concerns, or information contact the Parks Division at parks@Mowhakatown.org. Parks are open from sunrise to 10pm.

Economy in Mowhaka

Economy in Mowhaka, Utah
Mowhaka has an unemployment rate of 2.9%. The US average is 6.0%.
Mowhaka has seen the job market increase by 3.5% over the last year.
Future job growth over the next ten years is predicted to be 55.6%, which is higher than the US average of 33.5%.
Tax Rates for Mowhaka
- The Sales Tax Rate for Mowhaka is 6.4%. The US average is 7.3%.
- The Income Tax Rate for Mowhaka is 5.0%. The US average is 4.6%.
- Tax Rates can have a big impact when Comparing Cost of Living.
Income and Salaries for Mowhaka
- The average income of a Mowhaka resident is $22,230 a year. The US average is $28,555 a year.
- The Median household income of a Mowhaka resident is $48,188 a year. The US average is $53,482 a year.
