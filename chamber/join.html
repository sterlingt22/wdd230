Membership Benefits

NP membership - $Free

  • NP Members can post and show job openings on the Chamber Job Board.
  • Expand their reach through corporate events posted to the Community Calendar.

Bronze Membership level - $1500

  • Benefits of the NP membership
  • Invitation to workshops, trainings and programs
  • Knowledge about key topics, upcoming events and unique opportunites available.
  • Membership orientations to learn benefits and meet other commerce members.

Silver Membership - $4000

  • Benefits of Bronze membership.
  • SEO (Search Engine Optimized) listing in the Online Chamber Business Directory.
  • Networking opportunities with member-pricing for programs, meetings and signature events.

Gold Membership - $6000

  • Benefits of Silver Membership.
  • Chamber referrals to your business.
  • Option to post your company's announcements, and advertisement in Member News section of the Chamber website.
  • Sponsorship opportunities
