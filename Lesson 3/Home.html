Mowaka Chamber of Commerce

Site purpose

This site shows the current and upcoming festivals and a list of current businesses in Mowhaka. It shows the local attractions and vacation attraction hot spots. A for business owners section including places to advertise their business and post job openings. It has our local congratulatory section celebrating the quarterly business nominees for performing sensational business for its customers. Your opinion as a citizen of Mowhaka matters to us. Please contact us with any questions, concerns or ideas.
